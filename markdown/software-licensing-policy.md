## 1. Purpose & Scope

This policy defines the standards for **who**, **why**, and **when** software licenses are allocated within the organization. It ensures cost-effective, compliant, and efficient license distribution while minimizing unnecessary expenditures.

### üéØ Scope

| **Coverage Area** | **Details** |
|-------------------|-------------|
| **Applicable Users** | All employees, contractors, and third-party users requiring software access |
| **License Types** | Perpetual, subscription-based, and open-source licenses |
| **Exclusions** | Freeware (unless explicitly approved through this process) |

## 3. License Allocation Criteria

Before approving a license request, the following criteria must be systematically evaluated:

| **#** | **Criteria** | **Evaluation Guidelines** | **Priority** |
|-------|--------------|---------------------------|-------------|
| **1** | **Frequency of Use** | ‚Ä¢ High (Daily/Weekly) ‚Üí **Priority**<br>‚Ä¢ Medium (Monthly) ‚Üí **Review alternatives**<br>‚Ä¢ Low (Occasional) ‚Üí **Consider alternatives** | High |
| **2** | **Functional Necessity** | ‚Ä¢ Is the software **required** for core job duties?<br>‚Ä¢ Can existing enterprise tools fulfill the need?<br>‚Ä¢ Is this a "nice-to-have" vs. "must-have"? | Critical |
| **3** | **User Role/Department** | ‚Ä¢ Engineering vs. HR vs. Sales specific needs<br>‚Ä¢ Department budget allocation<br>‚Ä¢ Role-based access requirements | Medium |
| **4** | **Open-Source Alternatives** | ‚Ä¢ Is a free tool (LibreOffice, GIMP, etc.) sufficient?<br>‚Ä¢ Does the alternative meet security standards?<br>‚Ä¢ Training/migration costs considered? | High |
| **5** | **License Availability** | ‚Ä¢ Are unused licenses available in current inventory?<br>‚Ä¢ Can licenses be reassigned from inactive users?<br>‚Ä¢ Seasonal/temporary usage patterns? | Critical |
| **6** | **Supervisor Approval** | ‚Ä¢ Does the direct manager acknowledge **cost impact**?<br>‚Ä¢ Budget approval and business justification<br>‚Ä¢ Alternative solution consideration | Required |
| **7** | **Vendor Terms** | ‚Ä¢ Minimum seat requirements<br>‚Ä¢ Contract expiration dates<br>‚Ä¢ Volume discount opportunities | Medium |

### üìä Decision Matrix

| **Scenario** | **Action** | **Approval Level** | **Timeline** |
|--------------|------------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Existing license available** | Immediate allocation | IT Support | Same day |
| **Free alternative sufficient** | Recommend alternative | IT Support | 1-2 days |

### üéØ Key Decision Points

1. **Free Alternative Exists** ‚Üí Recommend unless business-critical functionality gap
2. **Infrequent Usage** ‚Üí Consider short-term access or shared licensing

## 7. Alternatives & Cost Optimization

### üÜì Recommended Free/Open-Source Alternatives

| **Commercial Software** | **Free/Open-Source Alternative** | **Compatibility** | **Use Case** | **Limitations** |
|-------------------------|----------------------------------|-------------------|--------------|----------------|
| **Microsoft Office** | LibreOffice / Google Workspace | 85% | Document creation, spreadsheets | Advanced Excel features |
| **Adobe Photoshop** | GIMP / Canva (Free Tier) | 70% | Basic image editing | Professional design tools |
| **Zoom Pro** | Microsoft Teams / Jitsi Meet | 90% | Video conferencing | Advanced webinar features |
| **Slack Premium** | Microsoft Teams / Discord | 80% | Team communication | Third-party integrations |
| **Tableau** | Power BI / Apache Superset | 75% | Data visualization | Advanced analytics |
| **AutoCAD** | FreeCAD / LibreCAD | 60% | Basic CAD work | Professional engineering |

### üí° Cost Optimization Strategies

1. **Volume Licensing** - Negotiate enterprise agreements for bulk purchases
2. **Annual vs. Monthly** - Choose annual subscriptions for 15-20% savings
3. **License Pooling** - Share licenses across departments for seasonal tools
4. **Regular Audits** - Identify and reallocate unused licenses quarterly
5. **Alternative Evaluation** - Always check [AlternativeTo.net](https://alternativeto.net) before purchasing
